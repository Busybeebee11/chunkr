Chunkr.io is the easiest way to split CSV and JSON files into multiple pieces. You upload a file, specify how you want it chunked up, and boom! We give you the chunks you asked for.

Our default data retention policy is for all content (uploaded files and output files) to be deleted 24 hours after the split is complete. Any deleted data is permanently removed and can not be recovered.
